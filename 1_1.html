About the Fisher-Men Rescue App

  • Real-time Tracking: Monitors the precise location of fishermen, ensuring timely interventions during emergencies.

  • Emergency Alerts: Instantly connects fishermen with emergency responders, coast guards, and nearby vessels at critical moments, enhancing rescue efforts.

  • User-friendly Interface: Simplifies navigation and access to emergency features, making it easy for users to operate even under stress.

  • Data Insights: Provides valuable data analytics to improve safety protocols and preventive measures.
