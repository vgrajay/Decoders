About the cvd-prediction

  • Hybrid Deep Learning: Utilizes a combination of CNN and RNN architectures to analyze both spatial and temporal data for accurate cardiovascular disease prediction.

  • Dual-Input Capability: Processes medical images like ECGs using CNN and time-series clinical data using RNN, enabling multi-dimensional analysis.

  • Improved Diagnosis Accuracy: Fusion of features from both models enhances prediction reliability, reducing false positives and false negatives.

  • Scalable and Trainable: The model can be trained on various datasets and extended to other medical conditions with minimal changes.

  • Real-world Applicability: Suitable for integration into hospital systems, mobile health apps, and diagnostic tools to assist medical professionals in early detection.
